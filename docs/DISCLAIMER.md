# Disclaimer — reference implementation

**golden-rabbit-engine** is offered as an **indicative open-source reference**: it maps narrative and formal concepts from the *Golden Rabbit* Volume 2 story to Go packages with unit tests.

It is **not**:

- A certified compliance framework or legal instrument  
- A complete SaaS or on-prem product  
- A substitute for your own threat modelling, data protection, and procurement reviews  

Integrations (LLM vendors, buses, databases, HSM/KMS, observability, identity, multi-tenant isolation at scale) are **intentionally out of scope** of this repository unless added explicitly by downstream projects.
